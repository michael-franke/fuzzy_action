Instructions

In this experiment, you will be shown six descriptions that involve a quantity of some sort (speed, price, duration, etc.). We are interested in your intuitive guess about that quantity, once you have read the description.

Some of the descriptions may sound more or less natural: please try to give your best guess anyway. After you have made a guess, we will also ask you to rate how natural the description sounds to you.

Example

Background story: Little Mike went to a friend's birthday party yesterday afternoon.

Description: Mike had an OK time.

Question: How much did little Mike enjoy the party?


This description suggests that the party was not a whole lot of fun. So we might rate it like this:
not at all enormously



Background story: Sue bought some flowers from a shop this morning.

Description: Sue thought the flowers were pretty.

Question: How much did Sue like the flowers?


The story suggests that she liked the flowers all right. So we might rate it like this:
not at all best thing ever


However, the exact rating is up to you — judge according to your own feeling, remembering that there are no right or wrong answers.
